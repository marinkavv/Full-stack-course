About SkyLine Aviation

Company history:

SkyLine Aviation was founded in 2008 by a group of aerospace engineers who believed that air travel could be safer and more sustainable. Over the years, the company has developed partnerships with major aircraft manufacturers and international airports.

Key achievements:

  • Opened 120+ international routes
  • Designed 3 eco-friendly aircraft prototypes
  • Trained over 5,000 pilots
  • Received 12 global aviation awards

Founder's quote:

“We don’t just build aircraft — we build the future of mobility.”
